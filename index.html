Alice Troy-Donovan - Fundraising and Access Support for individuals and charities

Access Support

I work with organisations to apply for funding if they have additional access barriers - for instance if staff are neurodivergent, or face health-related difficulties. Many funders offer access schemes and applicants typically have to dominate a support worker who will assist them with the application process. Feel free to reach out below for a conversation if you are looking for an access support worker!

I also support individual artists or organisations to apply for Arts Council funding through the Access Support scheme. I have previously supported neurodivergent artists to successfully secure funding through the Developing Your Creative Practice fund. I've also supported organisations to submit complex large bids for Arts Council Project Grants.

I start with a conversation to get to know you and your particular needs. I will then devise a tailored plan based on this. Support could look like note-taking, breaking down the application into manageable tasks, and helping you get your ideas on paper.

Fundraising Consultancy for Organisations

I offer a range of fundraising-related support to charitable organisations in the UK, with a particular interest in grassroots organising with a social justice focus. Support ranges from working with organisations to develop a persuasive case for support, to prospecting, research and compiling budgets for funders. I have a keen eye for detail, authenticity and argumentation and like to work flexibly with organisations to build sustainable funding sources.
