Access Support

I support individuals and organisations to apply for funding if they need additional support. I generally offer support to neurodivergent people who may face barriers, struggle with meeting deadlines or expressing their ideas in writing.

Some funders offer access schemes and applicants typically have to nominate a support worker who will assist them with the application process. If you're looking for assistance, please reach out for a conversation to see if I can support you!

I also work with individual artists or organisations to apply for Arts Council funding through the Access Support scheme. I have previously supported neurodivergent artists to successfully secure funding through the Developing Your Creative Practice fund. I've also supported organisations to submit complex large bids for Arts Council Project Grants.

How Does It Work?

I start with a conversation to get to know you and your particular needs. I will then devise a tailored plan based on this. Support could include:

* note-taking and transcription

* helping process the application and forms

* support navigating online funding portals

* signposting to other resources

Fundraising Consultancy

I offer a range of fundraising-related support to charitable organisations in the UK. Most of my experience is with grassroots and medium-sized charities and Community Interest Companies with a strong social justice approach.

I can work with you to find and apply for funding, develop a case for support, research new income streams, compile budgets for funders and more. I have a keen eye for detail, authenticity and argumentation and like to work flexibly with organisations to build sustainable funding sources.
